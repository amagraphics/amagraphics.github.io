ABOUT ME

My name is Adrià Martínez Alegret. I'm a graphic designer from Reus, Catalonia. I am passionate about graphic design and art in general. I am also interested in video games and web development.


As a kid, I always enjoyed creating stuff and playing video games, letting my imagination run wild. I quickly became interested in art and the fascinating world of design. After finishing secondary school, I studied art in high school and got a diploma. It was then, when I completely fell in love with graphic design.


The next year, I focused my studies on graphic design, and for two years on a row, I learned a lot of interesting things and I met a lot of awesome people. Since graduating from the design school, I've been working very hard everyday to improve as a designer. After all, that's all that matters!
